About Rainydays

Rainydays has over 20 years' experience with hiking clothing adapted to Nordic conditions. By placing emphasis on design and practical solutions, Rainydays has developed hiking clothing that allows everyone to be well dressed on small and large trips in all kinds of weather. The hiking clothes from Rainydays are sold at friendly prices. It shouldn't cost too much to get out on a trip together. Ever since its inception in 1998, Rainydays has taken a major social responsibility. 25% of all those recruited must be people who normally end up outside the workforce. Rainydays was the first sports and textile wholesaler to be included in the Initiative for Ethical Trade in 2002, and the company works continuously to reduce the climate and environmental impact associated with the business.
